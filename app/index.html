Getting There

Meadowland Farm can be found 80 miles north of New York City in Clinton Corners, accessible by train or car.

For the train, take either MTA Metro North from Grand Central Terminal to Poughkeepsie ($18) or the Amtrak from New York Penn Station to Rhinecliff ($30).

Uber & taxis are available at both destinations costing around $25 for a ride to the local area.

For rental cars, you can often get a cheaper deal by renting from the Hertz in White Plains & taking a short train there from Grand Central.
